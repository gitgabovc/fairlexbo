Our Legal Services

At Fairlex, we are committed to providing you with expert legal advice to resolve your issues

Intellectual Property

Today, Intellectual Property is the most valuable asset for both individuals and legal entities. FAIRLEXbo's services focus on providing comprehensive advice regarding the protection of these assets, their distinctiveness, and defense in national and international commerce.

Business Law

The constitution and organization of a company are the first steps for any venture that seeks to make a secure impact on the market and ensure its proper growth in accordance with current legal requirements.

FAIRLEXbo offers comprehensive advice on all the requirements for constitution and organization, providing the best options to our clients so they can make efficient decisions.

International Trade

Our 'foreign trade' service involves comprehensive guidance on import and export operations of various products. This service includes advice on authorizations, permits, tariff classification, preparation of declarations, and the calculation of the respective customs duties.

Likewise, it entails an immediate response to any customs-related dispute.

Pro Bono - Copyright Law

The assistance to others is one of the most important pillars of FAIRLEXbo, which demonstrates our quality as individuals. Consequently, with the support of various professionals specialized in Copyright Law, FARLEX has created a program to support our Bolivian authors, artists, and performers who require free legal advice in the face of any dispute that threatens their intellectual rights.
