Unauthorized Use of a Person's Image

Date: October 4, 2023

Written by: Fairlexbo

The misuse of a person's image is a crucial issue in the era of information and technology. This phenomenon occurs when an individual's image is used without their consent for various purposes, such as advertising, defamation, or identity manipulation. In this context, it is essential to understand the legal implications associated with this practice and how it affects a person's privacy rights.

In many countries, individuals have privacy and publicity rights that grant them control over the use of their image. These rights may vary by jurisdiction, but generally, they allow people to decide when and how their image is used in public or commercial contexts. Consent is a key part of this equation: if someone has not given their express permission for the use of their image, any unauthorized use can be considered a misuse.
